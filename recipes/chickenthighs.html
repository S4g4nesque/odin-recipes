Lau's Chicken Thighs

Juicy chicken thighs marinaded in a soy sauce and oyster sauce marinade.


A great tip from this chef is to start frying the chickin on high heat to get the crispiness on the outside, then bring it down to a low simmer to keep the juiciness when finished.

Ingredients:

  • 400g Chicken thighs
  • 2 onions
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • a thumb of ginger

How to cook it:

  1. Marinade the chicken for at least 30 min
  2. Bring the pan to a medium-high heat
  3. Place the chicken thighs so they don't touch each other
  4. Bring heat down to low-medium immediately
  5. Once the chicken is cooked half-way, bring the heat back up as you flip each piece over
  6. Once the pan is hot again, throw in the veg, add a splash of white wine, and cover with a lid
