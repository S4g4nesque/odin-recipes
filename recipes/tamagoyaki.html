Tamagoyaki

A traditional Japanese omelette seasoned with bonito stock.


This dish has a special place in my heart as it's an omelette my mother used to make for me frequently as a kid.
In Tokyo they like to add sugar for a touch of sweet, but since my mother is from Kansai we're going simple and savory.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tea-spoon bonito stock (powdered or liquid concentrate)
  • a handful chopped spring onions
  • 2 eggs

How to cook it:

  1. Heat a pan (rectangular if you have one) to medium heat
  2. Crack eggs in a bowl, add the stock and spring onions, and beat
  3. Pour half the eggs into the pan and fold about 3cm at a time
  4. Pour the rest of the eggs in and repeat
