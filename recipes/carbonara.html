Spaghetti alla Carbonara

Classic Roman dish that's filling and satisfying.


This classic Roman dish is easy to make and great to fill you up.

Ingredients:

  • 200g guanciale
  • black pepper
  • as much pecorino as you'd like
  • 1 egg per person, plus one yolk
  • 400g spaghetti

How to cook it:

  1. Heat the pan, but go ahead and put in the guanciale as it's heating to render the fat
  2. Boil water, salt, and throw the spaghetti in
  3. In a bowl, beat eggs, grate in some pecorino, and add a generous amount of pepper
  4. Once the pasta is done, toss it into the pan with the guanciale. *Make sure the pan is warm, not hot! Otherwise the eggs you put in will scramble.
  5. Stir in the egg "sauce" so it cooks ever so slightly with the residual heat
  6. Place into a bowl and enjoy!
